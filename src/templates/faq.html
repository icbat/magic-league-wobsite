A collection of Q's and their A's

Q: What's Standard Peasant?
A: 60 card minimum decks, using Standard legality, with no more than 5 uncommons; the rest must be common.
Q: Where can I find out what's in Standard right now?
A: The aptly named What's in Standard.com
Q: What happens with the new set in July?
A: No cards rotate out, Magic Origins becomes legal to add to your decks July 17th
Q: What if a card is printed at different rarities?
A: The card's rarity is based on it's printing in its standard-legal set. E.g. Ornithopter is considered common, despite being uncommon in every printing except M15, while Act of Treason is still a common despite its most recent printing being uncommon.
Q: Do I have to play my matches in person?
A: Nope! Feel free to use MTGO or Cockatrice to play, as long as your opponent is consenting.
Q: How are points earned?
A: A win is worth 3 points, a loss is worth 1 point. The unlikely tie is also worth 1 point. Each unique opponent played in a 3-week block is worth 2 points.
Q: When are the blocks?
A: 1st segment (Huey): June 5 - June 25
2nd segment (Dewey): June 26 - July 16
